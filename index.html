Mission Approach

Coming soon

Humanoid robots built for everyday life.

Pippin Robotics is building AI-powered humanoids for the homes, workplaces, and routines people already have.

Most robots ask you to adapt to them: new layouts, new workflows, new expectations. We are building Pippin to work within what already exists. Capable in ordinary spaces, safe around the people in them, and practical enough to become part of daily life.

Humanoids that work with people

Robots that meet you where you are.

Humanoid robots belong in the environments people already inhabit. We are building Pippin to work in those spaces alongside you, picking up on how you move and what you need rather than asking you to learn a new way of living or working.

Safe by design

Safety is not an afterthought.

A humanoid robot in your home or workplace has to feel like it belongs there. We are designing for predictable behavior, transparent limitations, and conservative defaults. Trust comes from a robot that respects the space and the people already in it.

Built for the real world

Capability that holds up outside the lab.

Pippin is designed for the physical world as it is: doorways, stairs, cluttered rooms, and shifting tasks. No custom floor plan. No dedicated lab. Just a humanoid built to navigate and work in the spaces you already use.

Learning by doing

Research and product, together.

We believe the best humanoid robotics comes from building in the real world, not around it. Products keep us grounded in how people actually live; rigorous engineering keeps us moving forward. We plan to share what we learn along the way.
